How to Repay a Loan
Self Repayment Plan

1. Go to MPESA Menu
2. Select Lipa Na Mpesa
3. Paybill Option
4. Enter Paybill No. 950468
5. Account No.: LOAN
6. Enter Amount
7. Enter PIN and confirm


Primapesa will be notified instantly, and when the transaction is succeful, your loan will be repaid.

Employer Repayment Plan

Your Loan can be repaid by your Employer. This is through deduction from your salary at the end of the month.
